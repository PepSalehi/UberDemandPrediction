Future Research Direction

The model could certainly benefit from a more significant number of samples over a longer duration (a full year) and the incorporation of additional ancillary data about the the region such as:

  • Holidays
  • Special Events
  • Weather
  • Days Since Opening of Market
  • Days Since New Product Launch (UberX)

The overall robustness and accuracy of demand prediction could be enhanced further by employing more sophisticated statistical models that can utilize continuous and categorical data such as Classification And Regression Tree (CART) Analysis or leverage a continuous update of information on a daily or hourly basis with a Bayesian approach like a Hidden Markov Model (HMM).
