Future Research Direction

The model could certainly benefit from a more significant number of samples over a longer duration (a full year) and the incorporation of additional ancillary data about the the region such as:

  • Holidays
  • Special Events
  • Weather
  • Days Since Opening of Market
  • Days Since New Product Launch (UberX)

The overall robustness and accuracy of demand prediction could be enhanced further by employing more sophisticated statistical models such as Classification And Regression Tree (CART) Analysis that can utilize continuous and catgorical data.
